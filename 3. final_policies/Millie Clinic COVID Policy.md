Millie Clinic COVID Policy

**GENERAL PREVENTION AND POLICIES**

1. All of our in clinic staff are required to be COVID vaccinated (& boostered when appropriate).
2. Masking is required in the clinic at all times, for staff and patients. We distribute masks upon entry if needed.
3. Cleaning and disinfection of clinic spaces will be performed regularly per CDC guidelines.
4. Maintain 6ft of distance when possible.
5. In the event that a COVID positive patient must be seen in person, it should occur when other patients are not in clinic, preferably at the end of work day.

**GUIDANCE FOR PATIENTS:**

**IF COVID POSITIVE:**

Isolation [per CDPH](https://www.cdph.ca.gov/Programs/CID/DCDC/Pages/COVID-19/Guidance-on-Isolation-and-Quarantine-for-COVID-19-Contact-Tracing.aspx): Stay home for *at least* 5 days. Reschedule all non-medically necessary in clinic apts to after isolation has ended. May schedule virtual apts.

* Please discuss with the clinician to determine if appointment is medically necessary or not.
* Isolation can end after day 5 (with continued mask wearing for a total of 10 days) IF:

a) asymptomatic or improving AND

b) a rapid antigen test collected on day 5 or later is negative AND

c) no fever

If unable to test or choosing not to test on or after day 5, and symptoms are gone or improving, isolation can end after day 10.

* Isolation timing: when you have COVID-19, isolation is counted in days, as follows:

If you had no symptoms

* Day 0 is the day you were tested (not the day you received your positive test result)
* Day 1 is the first full day following the day you were tested
* If you develop [symptoms](https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/symptoms-testing/symptoms.html) within 10 days of when you were tested, the clock restarts at day 0 on the day of symptom onset

If you had symptoms

* Day 0 of isolation is the day of symptom onset, regardless of when you tested positive
* Day 1 is the first full day after the day your [symptoms](https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/symptoms-testing/symptoms.html) started

**IF COVID EXPOSURE OR COVID SYMPTOMS & UNKNOWN RESULT:**

Take a rapid antigen test at home or a clinician can order a PCR test to the lab and await results.

If negative → OK to schedule in clinic apts. Require patient wears N95, clinic will provide.

If positive → follow the above steps under “IF COVID POSITIVE”

* If asymptomatic, schedule virtual apt w/ clinician within 72hrs
* If symptomatic, schedule virtual apt w/ clinician within 24hrs
