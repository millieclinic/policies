# Breach Notification — Media Press Release Template

> Use this template under the direction of the Privacy Officer when a Breach involves 500 or more residents of a single State. Legal counsel and the cyber liability insurance carrier (if engaged) must review before distribution. Content must mirror the information provided in individual notices (see `breach-notification-individual-letter.md`). See `incident-and-breach-response.md` §III.7.4 for distribution requirements.
